April Project Artificial Turf Calculator

Objective

Problem

Hill Horticulture Inc. is a landscaping company in San Antonio. They do all types of landscaping, but the most prominent service is artificial turf installations. While listening to calls and feedback from the client, we discovered a few reoccurring themes.

  1. High volume of unqualified leads
  2. Sticker shock from the price of artificial turf
  3. Knowledge of all artificial turf benefits

We already started writing more blog to educate visitors on artificial turf but needed a solution to give value to artificial turf to combat the sticker shock and increase the percentage of leads that are qualified before reaching sales.

Solution

The artificial turf calculator helps the sales team understand more about the lead as well as give the lead understand the cost of artificial turf. The calculator also determines how much you spend on lawn maintenance per year and puts it into perspective the the visitor of how much money and time they spend on the lawn.

What I Accomplished

Research

  • Researching factors that contribute to the cost of lawn maintenance.
  • Researching all costs of turf including the turf itself and an installation.
  • Putting together an equation for turf cost and an equation for lawn maintenance cost per year.
  • Wireframe of what I want the page to look like.

Coding

  • HTML: I used bootstrap to make the page mobile responsive. It was easy to set up but the HTML code is cluttered.
  • CSS using SASS: I used SASS to create the page which increased my speed by at least 50%. It was easy to change out colors with variables and create a responsive site with mixins.
  • jQuery: I used jQuery as my Javascript library. This part of the project took up 80% of my time. I learned a lot of valuable code from this section but also found the parts of Javascript to work on.
